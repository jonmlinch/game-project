That Killa Thrilla Gorilla Surf Game

Directions:
As every knows gorillas can't surf. But get a gorilla drunk and it becomes a whole other story. In this game, you are playing as a surfing Gorilla trying to maintain his drunkenness and so he can keep surfing. The rules are simple:
  • Use the arrow keys to move your gorilla around the wave.
  • Collect rum bottles to increase drunkenness but watch out for tuber! They decrease it.
  • Get 100% drunk and you win. Lose all your buzz and you'll wipeout because sober gorillas don't surf.
